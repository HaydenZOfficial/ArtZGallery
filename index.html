03 Who let the dog code?

A LITTLE
ABOUT THE MUTT.

I’m Limonene. I build websites that feel handmade, expressive, and a little unruly.

My favorite projects start with somebody’s art, colors, characters, community, and personality—then turn all of that into a space they can actually own. I care about readable layouts, mobile support, and giving artists control over their own work.

This portfolio is also an experiment: part résumé, part digital den, part proof that the web can still be strange.

“MAKE THE INTERNET PERSONAL AGAIN.”
