Before you submit

Give me enough to understand the vision.

You do not need professional art terminology. Character references, sketches, playlists, color palettes, and plain descriptions are useful.

Payment verification

Selecting “I already paid” does not automatically prove payment. It marks the request as payment claimed. Haden will check PayPal or Ko-fi and manually change it to paid — verified.

  • Explain what you want drawn.
  • Include reference links when possible.
  • State personal or commercial use.
  • Mention a deadline before booking.
  • Do not include passwords or card information.

What happens next

A request is not a charge.

Haden reviews the request privately, discusses the scope with you, and confirms payment separately before changing the request to paid.

  1. 01

    Request received

    Your message appears in Haden’s private website inbox with a reference number.

  2. 02

    Scope reviewed

    Haden may contact you for references, changes, timing, usage, or a final quote.

  3. 03

    Payment verified

    After checking the actual PayPal or Ko-fi transaction, Haden marks the inbox record as paid — verified.

  4. 04

    Work begins

    The request can then move through accepted, in progress, and completed statuses.
