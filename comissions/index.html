02 Creative boundaries

KNOW THE
LIMITS

A clear brief makes the process faster. Read both files before submitting, especially for mature, violent, political, or commercial material.

✓
ACCEPTED SUBJECTS

GREEN FILE

  • Humans, kemonomimi, hybrids, and original characters
  • Anthro characters, furries, creatures, animals, and ferals
  • Monsters, fantasy designs, robots, mecha, and armor
  • Suggestive or adult work with proper age verification
  • Light gore and dramatic horror imagery
×
DECLINED SUBJECTS

RED FILE

  • Long-term comic or animation production commitments
  • Sexual violence, coercion, exploitation, or any sexual content involving minors
  • Heavy or extreme gore
  • Hateful content or discriminatory propaganda
  • NFT use, AI training, or dataset use of commissioned artwork

03 Agreement protocol

READ BEFORE
TRANSMISSION

Submitting the form starts a conversation, not an automatic acceptance. Final scope, price, schedule, and payment details are confirmed directly.

01

Request review

Your brief is reviewed for subject, scope, schedule, and fit. A request can be declined before payment or work begins.

02

Quote and payment

The final quote may differ from the starting prices. Do not send payment until the artist confirms the request and payment instructions.

03

Creation and revisions

Provide complete references early. Major changes after approval may require a new quote or added fee.

04

Usage and credit

Personal use is the default unless commercial usage is discussed. The artwork may not be used for NFTs or AI training.
